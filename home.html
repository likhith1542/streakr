Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Designed with one goal — help you show up every day.

🔥
Streak tracking
Current streak, best streak, and at-risk warnings so you never break a chain by accident.
📅
Smart frequency
Daily, specific days of the week, or X times per week. Rest days don't break your streak.
📊
History & heatmaps
GitHub-style activity heatmaps and a monthly calendar. Click any day to edit history.

Stats & charts
Completion rate, 14-day trends, and a habit leaderboard to see what's sticking.

Dark & light mode
Warm dark theme or crisp light mode. Follows your system or set it manually.

Export & import
Download a full JSON backup anytime. Restore it on a new machine in one click.

Privacy

Your data lives
on your Mac.

No account. No server. No analytics. Streakr is a local app — your habits are stored in a JSON file on your own machine and nowhere else.

🔒
No account required
Open the app and start immediately. We don't know you exist.
📁
Local JSON storage
Data lives at ~/Library/Application Support/com.streakr.habittracker/
📤
Full export anytime
Download a JSON backup and import it on any new machine.
